Artist Bio
CoffeeBag is the project of Saksham Dhingra — a producer, DJ, and studio owner driven by the tension between raw mechanical energy and melodic warmth. Operating from Layalok Music Studio in Panipat, Haryana, his productions straddle underground Techno and the percussive pulse of Afro House, filtered through a uniquely Indian sensibility.

Trained in Sound Engineering at ILM Gurgaon and holding a Diploma in AI Mastery (2025), Saksham brings both technical rigour and forward-thinking production methods to CoffeeBag. His workflow spans Ableton Live and Pro Tools, with a creative practice that increasingly fuses generative AI tools with traditional sound design.


As the founder of Layalok Music Studio — his own production space in Panipat — Saksham has built an environment dedicated entirely to electronic music creation, from sound design and mixing to live performance rehearsal and finger drumming.


His Bollywood electronic remixes bridge the gap between the Indian mainstream and global club culture — reframing familiar melodies through driving kick patterns and wide, hypnotic pads. It's a sound that feels both immediately recognisable and entirely new.
